A compel is the dark side of the [[invoke]]. Where an invoke costs a Fate point but gives a bonus (either a reroll or a flat bonus to the result) a compel is somewhat of the opposite. If an Aspect makes a [[skill test]] narratively impossible then it can be compelled. This includes scene Aspects as well as character Aspects. If compelled the skill test automatically fails and the character is awarded 1 Fate point. A compel can be averted by paying a Fate point or by challenging a Character Aspect if the compel was made one a Character Aspect. Once averted a skill test can be performed as usual.
