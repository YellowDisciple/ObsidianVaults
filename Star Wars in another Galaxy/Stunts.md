###### Mandalorian Phalanx
*You have trained to stand in the Mandalorian Phalanx with an Energy Aspis and a Laser Spear. Protecting yourself and your fellow Mandalorians from incoming Attacks with the Energy Aspis.*
While you are equipped with an Energy Aspis, you can use Athletics+Fight to [[Skill test#Defend|Defend]] against incoming energy weapon Attacks.
###### Personal Shield Generator
*You have salvaged a particularly potent shield generator, maybe from a fighter or hardened battle droid. While its energy drain is far too large for your systems to run continuously, you can turn it on for the split seconds of incoming energy based Attacks, to block them effectively.*
While you have your Personal Shield Generator on you, you can use Craft+Athletics to [[Skill test#Defend|Defend]] against incoming energy weapon Attacks.
###### Always making useful things
*You have an uncanny knack for crafting things and your hands hardly ever stay still for long.*
Even in the most unlikely of circumstances you can craft scene appropriate useful tools and gadgets even if deprived of your usual equipment.
###### Sniper
*Years of action all across the galaxy have turned your hands as steady as those of a droid*
When using Athletics+Shoot to [[Skill test#Create an Advantage|Create an Advantage]] successfully, you add an additional free invocation to the Aspect.
###### Jawa Eyes
*You have the infrared vision of your people. Darkness is nothing to you*
You ignore any penalties to Skill tests that stem from Darkness related Aspects. Furthermore these aspects cannot deny you a related vision based action and cannot be [[compel|compelled]] against you in relation to vision.
###### Scrapicle
*You have an extensive database of scrap merchants, and junkyard dealers.*
You can substitute Investigate for Contacts when creating a Contact with the [[Skill test#Create an Advantage|Create an Advantage]] Skill test which relates to Technology.
