This section explains the rules of this adaptation of the Fate System Toolkit and the 2d20 SRD for the Homebrew setting [[Star Wars in another Galaxy]].
The game is played by a Storyteller or Game Maser (GM) and several players. The Game Master controls the narrative of the world and the players control the actions of their characters in this world. Each player's character is formalized in a [[character sheet]]. 

When a character wishes to act upon the world but the success of the action is in doubt, the player of the character performs am [[Action economy#Action|Action]], which leads into a  [[Skill test]] to resolve the issue.
